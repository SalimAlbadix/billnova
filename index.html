How can we help?

Simple, fast invoicing for your business. Get answers to common questions below.

How do I create an invoice?

Tap the + button on the home screen, fill in your client details and line items, then tap Save or Send.

Can I customize my invoice template?

Yes. Go to Settings and choose from the available templates. You can add your logo and adjust colors.

How do I export my invoices?

Open any invoice and tap the share icon to export as PDF or send directly via email.

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your App Store account settings.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings, tap Account, then tap Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.
