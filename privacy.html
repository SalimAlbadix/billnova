Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

BillNova ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Invoice Data: Client names, addresses, line items, and amounts that you enter to create invoices.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about how you interact with the app to help us improve the experience.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's invoicing features.
  • To process your account registration and manage your subscription.
  • To send you important updates about your account or changes to our services.
  • To improve and optimize the app experience.
  • To respond to your support requests.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use secure servers and take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only when required by law or to protect our rights.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the app by going to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Upon deletion, all your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

BillNova is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the date above.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@albadi.io.
